In the blue you love, so deep and clear,
I stand, a shadow waiting here,
Not to block the light but just to be,
A presence, quiet, by your side to see.
If I could be the shade you find,
Not leading you but close behind,
Would you embrace this place I stay,
In the blue that paints your way?

And when my voice like sunset fades,
Reaching you through softer shades,
It’s not to heal, but walk beside,
In steps of hope and strength you bide.
The path you take is yours alone,
Yet I’ll walk with you, stone by stone.
Through thorns and shadows, you’ll get through.

On nights when burdens weigh too much,
You hold my hand with the gentlest touch,
Soft as powder, warm as light,
In your grip, my doubts take flight.
With stubborn heart, with steadfast eyes,
There’s fear in you but strength in size,
Your gaze on me, a vow you keep,
And in that vow, my spirit leaps.

It’s not to heal, but walk beside,

You love the blue, the music’s call!
You rise above, you stand so tall!
Rock and soul in power and song,
Your voice the place where hearts belong.
In the world you paint in shades of blue,
With every pain and joy held true,
I watch in pride, in silent awe,
The world you color, raw and pure.
